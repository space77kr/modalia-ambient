# Blue Skies - Image Prompt

## Version

1.0

## Status

Draft

---

# Objective

Create a premium album cover that captures the uplifting and peaceful feeling of "Blue Skies."

The artwork should feel timeless, elegant, and emotionally warm.

---

# Master Prompt

A peaceful summer landscape beneath a brilliant blue sky with soft white clouds, warm golden morning sunlight, a gentle breeze moving through a green meadow filled with delicate wildflowers, cinematic natural lighting, dreamy atmosphere, minimal composition, premium album cover design, realistic photography style, ultra high resolution, soft color grading, clean aesthetic, emotional and calming mood.

---

# Negative Prompt

No people

No buildings

No city

No text

No logo

No watermark

No dark weather

No heavy shadows

No oversaturated colors

No fantasy elements

---

# Style Keywords

- Cinematic
- Natural
- Dreamy
- Minimal
- Premium
- Peaceful
- Soft Light
- Summer Morning

---

# Recommended Format

Aspect Ratio: 1:1

Resolution: 3000 × 3000 px or higher

---

Use this prompt as the foundation for future cover variations while keeping the visual identity consistent.
