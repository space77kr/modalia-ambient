# Concept

The cover should express the feeling of a bright summer morning filled with hope, freedom, and gentle happiness.

It should feel clean, airy, and emotionally uplifting.

- Soft white clouds
- Warm sunlight

- Gentle breeze
- Wildflowers
- Peaceful atmosphere
- Dreamy light
- Minimal composition

# Mood

- Bright

- Peaceful
- Dreamy
- Positive

# Style Direction

Natural and cinematic.

Avoid overly artificial or heavily saturated images.

Focus on simplicity, emotional warmth, and open space.
