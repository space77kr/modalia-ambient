# Master Prompt

Bright dream pop with crystal-clear youthful female vocals, airy sparkling tone and smiling vocal delivery. Catchy melodic hook within the first 8 seconds. Sparkling piano, clean acoustic guitar, subtle ukulele accents, warm bass, organic percussion, gentle hand claps, glockenspiel, ambient pads and soft strings. Emotional build toward an uplifting final chorus with layered harmonies. Polished modern production, wide stereo image, warm analog character, commercial-quality mix, memorable melody, feel-good summer atmosphere.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid heavy EDM drops.

Avoid aggressive rock guitars.

Avoid distorted vocals.

Avoid dark cinematic atmosphere.

Avoid lo-fi production.

Avoid rap or spoken vocals.

Avoid excessive vocal effects.

Avoid overly busy arrangements.

---

# A&R Notes

Primary hook should appear within the first 8 seconds.

The chorus must be instantly memorable.

Keep the arrangement open and uncluttered.

Prioritize emotional connection over technical complexity.

The vocal should always remain the focal point.
