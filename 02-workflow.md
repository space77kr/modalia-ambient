# Modalia Ambient Production Workflow

## Purpose

This workflow ensures that every song follows the same high-quality production process.

---

# Step 1 — A&R Planning

Define:

- Song title
- Concept
- Genre
- Mood
- BPM
- Key
- Target audience
- Reference songs

Output:

- A&R Notes

---

# Step 2 — Lyrics

Create

- Verse
- Pre-Chorus
- Chorus
- Bridge

Output:

lyrics.md

---

# Step 3 — Suno Prompt

Create the final Suno prompt.

Output:

suno-prompt.md

---

# Step 4 — Music Generation

Generate several versions.

Example

- V1
- V2
- V3
- V4

Choose the best version.

---

# Step 5 — Quality Review

Evaluate

- Melody
- Vocal
- Arrangement
- Hook
- Commercial appeal

---

# Step 6 — Cover Art

Create album artwork.

---

# Step 7 — Video

Create

- Lyric video
- Music video
- Shorts

---

# Step 8 — Release

Publish

- YouTube
- Spotify
- Apple Music

---
